\section*{Highlights}

\begin{itemize}
  \item OPENER assembles frozen pretrained components into an open-world NER pipeline
  \item Light contrastive fine-tuning with hard-negative mining sharpens the embedder
  \item Zero-shot and supervised typing heads share one frozen embedding space
  \item A 13-dataset benchmark jointly measures accuracy, latency and energy use
  \item Best zero-shot accuracy end-to-end with several times less energy than rivals
\end{itemize}
